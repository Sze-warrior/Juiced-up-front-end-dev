About Juiced Up:

Juiced Up is a fresh and lively juice bar located in central Wellington. With our bars located all around Wellington, you'd be a fool to miss out on one of our delicous drinks. Our drinks come in a wide variety for you to choose from. From using bananas, carrots, oranges and other wonderful fruits we have all that is needed to make a drink worth your time and money.

What makes us an attractive and unique place to buy a juice from is our trendy glassjars. We understand that you're always on the move in this busy world and that's why we came prepared with our trendy jars. Our hip cups are perfect to drink or store away while moving around. What's even better is the jar is yours when you take your order away or sit at our bar for a drink.

Take a look at our menu and see what takes your taste to get juiced up.
